About the Scholarship

The Dr. John G. Harris Memorial is a privately endowed scholarship fund dedicated to the memory of Dr. John G. Harris. Scholarships are awarded on a competitive basis to medical, nursing, and public health students and resident or fellow trainees purshing research related to oncology, palliative care, or geriatrics.
